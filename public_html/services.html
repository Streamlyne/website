What we do

Streamlyne will replace the current paper based system and provide the manufacturing industry with the ability to seamlessly transition into the mobile-based era. Our vision is to enable an employee sitting in front of a piece of equipment to have instant access to all information involved in its manufacturing, maintenance, and repair. The employee will have the ability to interact within the application providing and sharing annotations across their whole network.
