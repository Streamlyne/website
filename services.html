What we do

Streamlyne will replace the current paper based system and provide the manufacturing industry with the ability to seamlessly transition into the mobile-based era. Our vision is to enable an employee sitting in front of a piece of equipment to have instant access to all information involved in its manufacturing, maintenance, and repair. The employee will have the ability to interact within the application providing and sharing annotations across their whole network.

Annotations

The Streamlyne platform is centered on the concept of improving communication and access to information throughout the company. The ability to interact within 3D/2D CAD and images creates a network of information available to all necessary employees. The concept is to allow employees the ability to provide annotations on individual pieces within the CAD drawings and have others able to view and comment on them. This is designed to create a level of traceability, improve the available information around each component and dramatically improve the speed of information.

Safety

Maybe the most exciting long-term benefit of our platform is the impact on safety. The use of our application will create traceability and accountability allowing companies to ensure their employees are properly trained and that safety regulations are met. By establishing independent communication networks for each of our customers we are able to collect real time data. This enables companies to track issues or patterns in maintenance and repair, allowing them to act swiftly to address such issues before they escalate.

Maintenance and Repair

The worst thing that can happen to a manufacturing line is for there to be a shutdown in operation. The content of our platform breaks down the manufacturing process in such a way every specific component can be identified and isolated. This provides the employee with specific details on how each component is put together as well as showing them typical maintenance issues that may arise and even detailed repair techniques.

Manuals

Our platform will take all of the large, complicated manuals and make them available instantly to employees through the application. No longer will there be a need to flip back and forth through giant paper manuals searching for specific details. The complete manuals will now be accessed remotely with time saving search functionality built in to ensure employees are able to find what they need, when they need it.

Projects

Companies will be able to break down their workload into individual projects within the platform. This will allow workers of each product line instant access to the specific information they need to do their jobs well. All necessary information can be housed within each project including any 3D/2D CAD, important images and links to important instructional content.
