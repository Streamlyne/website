Our Platform

iOS Mobile platform and Web App
Our management analytics and employee application is built natively for iPad, iPad Mini, and iPhone. We also provide a web companion for managers and employees to use at their desktop. Employees working in demanding and tight spaces now have the ability to access necessary information on multiple devices.We also integrate with Asset Management Systems such as IBM Maximo and SAP.


Interactive 3D/2D CAD Profile
No more screen clutter or looking through mountains of paperwork and diagrams. Our platform allows employees to easily search, view, and interact with any part or component in 3D/2D CAD, such as: video/voice/text annotations, timeline history, procedures, work orders, operating parameters, manuals, photos/videos, and training. Our innovative technology will sync all this together in one profile.


Employee/Operator App
Our beautifully designed operator app is simple and easy to use with features, such as: Dashboard - quick access to all information; Inbox - send and receive messages to employees and managers; Groups - creates collaborative groups for your shift or anyone that is important to your job; Parts - your interactive 3D/2D CAD parts profile; Manuals - easily view detailed and complicated manuals and documents.


Management Analytics
Our platform generates real time insights and analytics of your employees, product and plant operations.Information tracking possibilities are vast and cover many different areas such as: onsite daily productivity, identify bottlenecks in product assembly, maintenance and repair cost analysis, part and component analysis down to the nut and bolt, and other actions and insights.
