Our Platform

iOS Mobile platform and Web App
Our management analytics and employee application is built natively for iPad, iPad Mini, and iPhone. We also provide a web companion for managers and employees to use on their desktop. Employees working in demanding and tight spaces now have the ability to access necessary information on multiple devices with a few simple clicks.


Interactive CAD Parts/Components Profile
Employees and managers can easily add video, voice, and text annotations directly in your 3D/2D CAD diagrams, images, and process flow diagrams. These can be shared through out their network, providing information and collecting feedback.


Employee/Opertor App
Our goal is to improve the way companies and employees are able to communicate with each other. From internal notification to companywide alerts, the value of a real time, systematic communication platform cannot be understated.


Management Analytics
Our platform generates real time insights and analytics of your employee’s and product and plant operations. Our platform can passively track employee’s onsite day to day productivity, identify bottlenecks in product assembly, maintenance and repair cost analysis, part and component analysis down to the nut and bolt, and many other actions and insights.
