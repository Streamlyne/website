Increase Productivity

Improve employee workflow and efficiency by replacing the dated, paper based system and interact with your 3D/2D CAD diagrams, images, manuals, procedures anywhere in your plant from one platform.

Increase Safety

Creating traceability and accountability in meeting safety and compliance standards. Ensuring accurate, update to date information on training, maintenance, and repairs.

Simple to Use

Developed from the ground up with feedback from manufacturing plants, Streamlyne has a simple learning curve that easily integrates into your employee’s daily workflow.

We want to simplify and streamline manufacturing employees daily workflow and make it easy for them be great at their jobs.

Our Platform

iOS Mobile platform
Built natively for iPad, iPhone, and iPad mini. Employees working in demanding and tight spaces now have the ability to access necessary information on the fly with simple “one touch” technology.


Annotations
Employees and managers can easily add video, voice, and text annotations directly in your 3D/2D CAD diagrams, images, and process flow diagrams. These can be shared through out their network, providing information and collecting feedback.


Communication
Our goal is to improve the way companies and employees are able to communicate with each other. From internal notification to company wide alerts, the value of a real time, systematic communication platform can not be understate.


Manuals
The ability to upload complicated manuals provides employees with easy and instant access at all times from any location necessary. The simplicity of our application allows for one click interaction and viewing on 3D/2D CAD and images.
